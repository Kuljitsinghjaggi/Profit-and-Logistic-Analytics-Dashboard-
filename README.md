Profit And Logistics Analytics 

Project Overview
This Power BI Command Center provides a high-level diagnostic of a multi-regional Superstore’s performance.
The project moves beyond basic sales tracking to analyze the critical intersection of Net Profitability, Logistics Efficiency, and Operational Risk.
By transforming 5,900+ raw transactional records into interactive visual intelligence, this dashboard enables stakeholders to identify profit engines,
optimize shipping modes, and mitigate revenue leakage from product returns.

Key Features & Insights
Executive KPI Scorecard: Real-time tracking of Total Sales, Profit Margins, and Order Quantities.
Logistics Performance: Comparative analysis of Shipping Modes (Standard, First Class, etc.) to evaluate fulfillment speed vs. cost.
Risk Management: A dedicated Returns Analysis module to pinpoint problematic product lines and regional quality issues.
Geospatial Intelligence: Interactive mapping of performance across 4 major US Regions to identify market penetration opportunities.
Payment Dynamics: Segmentation by Payment Mode (Online, Cards, COD) to monitor modern consumer purchasing trends.

Tech Stack
Data Visualization: Power BI Desktop
Data Source: Superstore Sales Dataset (CSV)
Design Theme: Executive Dark / Neon UI (Glassmorphism inspired)
Concepts Used: Data Modeling, DAX (Data Analysis Expressions), Geospatial Mapping, and Conditional Formatting.

Business Value Delivered
Profitability Optimization: Identified Technology as the highest-margin category, providing a roadmap for inventory reinvestment.
Operational Efficiency: Discovered correlations between specific shipping modes and high return frequencies, allowing for logistics policy adjustments.
Customer Insights: Highlighted a shift toward Online/Card payments, suggesting a need for streamlined digital checkout optimization.
